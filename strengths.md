CLIFTON STRENGTHS ASSESSMENT  
1.  CONSISTENCY:  You are keenly aware of the need to treat people the same; you set clear rules and follow them.   
2.  HARMONY:  You look for consensus and avoid conflict.
3.  RELATOR:  You enjoy close relationships and working with people you know well.  
4.  MAXIMIZER:  You focus on strengths as a way to stimulate excellence, preferring to refine strong areas.
5.  DELIBERATIVE:  You are careful and vigilant; you anticipate obstacles and think things through.
