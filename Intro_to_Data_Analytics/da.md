**INTRO TO DATA ANALYTICS** 

**Course Description:**   
Assess data quality and characteristics and apply descriptive and diagnostic techniques to provide insight and answers to
business questions. Appropriately communicate findings. Microsoft Excel will be used extensively.   

**Key Takeaways:**   
This course introduced me to Power Query in Excel, which helped strengthen my data cleaning skills and allowed me to create new columns using functions such as IFS, COUNTIFS, and XLOOKUP.  It also taught me how to build pivot tables and charts to add visualizations to my analysis, as well as different ways to present findings, such as using percentages instead of raw values.
