**Business Analysis Fundamentals**   

***Course Description:***   
The Business Analysis course includes instruction in the multiple approaches used for performing business
analysis, planning activities and ongoing communication, defining scopes, process improvements, assumptions,
constraints, and dependencies, and the management process.

***Key Takeaways***   
My key takeaways from this course mainly focus on understanding the process of managing a business project.  One important concept I learned was the value of using a SWOT analysis to conduct an initial evaluation of a project.  Secondly, a project can be approached in two ways: a predictive approach, which emphasizes control and risk reduction, or an adaptive approach, which allows for flexibility and continuous adjustments to find the best solution.  Another key takeaway was the importance of stakeholder engagement.  Without their support and guidance, a project can easily stall.  Therefore, it is crucial to keep stakeholders informed of any major updates, changes, or challenges throughout the project to ensure alignment and continued support.
