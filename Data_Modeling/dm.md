**DATA MODELING**   

**Course Description:**   
Learn how data modeling is used to manage data within an organization's business environment. Explore the model
design framework using an entity-relationship diagram and by building conceptual, logical, and physical data models
that conform to business needs and requirements. Discover why dimensional data models are a useful tool in business
intelligence applications.
