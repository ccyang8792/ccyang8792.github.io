**DATA GOVERNANCE**   

***Course Description:***   
This course will introduce you to specific ideas around how we manage and take care of data within organizations to make it as effective, reliable, accessible, and reusable as possible.  Many of these ideas are also covered and interwoven into other program courses.      

***Key Takeaways:***   
Honestly, this was one of the tougher courses for me within this program.  It seems to me that for someone to fully understand data governance, they must have a strong understanding of how data flows through an organization, such as how it's stored, secured, and used.  They must also know data policies and regulations that ensure data quality and maintain compliance.
